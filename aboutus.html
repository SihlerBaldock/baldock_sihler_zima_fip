We're Back... and Better!

That’s right! You’re favourite citrus drink from back in the day is back, and here to stay. With the launch and start of the new rebranding, the ZIMA team should set a few things straight.

Firstly, ZIMA was previously known to be a beverage that contains alcohol, and while that will still be an option to those who are curious, it will not be the main focus of the new ZIMA brand. Moving forward, ZIMA will focus on it’s brand new line of citrus soda beverages, with 5 new flavours to showcase!

Secondly and most important, the ZIMA team has worked hard and spent a caring amount of time to lay out some important brand guidelines and goals, in order to ensure that the ZIMA brand will remain trendy and successful.
